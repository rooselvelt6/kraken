# Governance

Kraken follows a **BDFL + Maintainers** governance model.

## Roles

### BDFL (Benevolent Dictator for Life)
The project creator serves as BDFL with final authority on:
- Project vision and roadmap
- Major architectural decisions
- Maintainer appointments

### Maintainers
Maintainers are responsible for day-to-day project management:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues
- Managing releases
- Enforcing community standards

### Contributors
Anyone who submits a pull request, files a bug report, or participates in discussions.

## Decision making

1. **Technical decisions**: Made by maintainers through lazy consensus (48h for objections)
2. **Roadmap decisions**: Made by the BDFL after community discussion
3. **Governance changes**: Require BDFL approval
